Navigation

The Liziba Light Rail passing through a building is a unique urban feature in Chongqing, where a light rail line actually runs through a high-rise residential building. For the locals, this is the route they take as part of their daily commute between the cities for work and life. The Line 2 was built in 2005, so this "sight" is already quite ordinary for the locals. It's worth noting that this line travels between the river and the mountains, and the scenery along the way should not be missed.

Transportation

How to get there

The best way to get to Liziba is by taking the light rail, which allows you to personally experience the famous phenomenon of the light rail passing through a building.

Nearby

Explore the Area

Following the mountain path behind the light rail station will lead you to a new attraction built on the site of the former Chongqing Mint. Climbing to the top of the mountain also offers some nice views. However, make sure not to get lost.
