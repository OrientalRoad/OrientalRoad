Taiyuan

Shanxi Province, North China
Heritage Sites
Renowned historic and cultural city
240-Hour Transit Visa (Taiyuan, Datong)

Intro

The city of Taiyuan is surrounded by mountains on its east, west, and north sides, while the central and southern parts consist of the Fen River valley plain. The overall terrain slopes from higher in the north to lower in the south.

NameTaiyuan
ClimateWarm temperate continental monsoon climate
Area6,988 km²
Population5 million
GeographyCentral to northern Shanxi, in the northern part of the Taiyuan Basin
