Yangzhou

Jiangsu Province, East China

Rich cultural heritage

240-Hour Transit Visa (Jiangsu Province)

Intro

Yangzhou is a historic city known for its classical gardens, rich culture, and delicious cuisine. Once a prosperous hub along the Grand Canal, Yangzhou offers visitors a glimpse into ancient Chinese history and traditions.

NameYangzhou
ClimateMild subtropical climate.
Area6,634 km²
Population5 million
GeographySituated on the north bank of the Yangtze River, near the Grand Canal.
