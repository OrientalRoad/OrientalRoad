Island

240-Hour Transit Visa (Zhejiang Province)

Intro

Zhoushan is the largest archipelago city in China, renowned for its beautiful island scenery. The Zhoushan Archipelago consists of 2085 islands, among which the most famous are Putuo Mountain, Zhujiajian, Taohua Island, Daishan Island, Shengsi Islands, and Dongji Island.
